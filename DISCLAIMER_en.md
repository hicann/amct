# Disclaimer

## To cann-amct Users
If you encounter any issues while using cann-amct (including but not limited to functional issues and compliance issues), please submit an issue on GitCode. We will review and resolve it promptly.

## To Dataset Owners
If you do not want your dataset to be mentioned in the examples of the cann-amct repository, or if you wish to update the description of your dataset in the model examples, please submit an issue on GitCode. We will delete or update your dataset description according to your issue requirements. We sincerely thank you for your understanding and contribution to cann-amct.
